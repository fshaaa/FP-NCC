ABOUT

THE GAME

Genshin Impact is an open-world action ARPG developed by miHoYo. Everything begins in a fantasy land named Teyvat, where the chosen ones have been granted Visions by the Archons and become able to wield the power of the seven elements. You, a mysterious traveler, is about to embark on epic adventures in this free world, where amazing friends with various abilities will join you along the way. They will help you fight intimidating enemies, find your lost kin, and unravel all the mysteries of the world of Genshin Impact. The game is officially announced for PC, PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch, Android and iOS

PC MINIMUM

Operating System: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit Processor: Intel Core i5 or equivalent Memory: 8 GB RAM Graphics Card: nVidia® GeForce® GT 1030 and higher DirectX Version: 11 or higher Storage: Please reserve 30 GB of space

PC RECOMMENDED

Operating System: Windows 7/8/10 64-bit Processor: Intel Core i7 equivalent or higher Memory: 16 GB RAM Graphics: nVidia GeForce GTX 1060 6 GB and higher DirectX Version: 11 or higher Storage: Please reserve 30 GB of space

CONSOLE AND MOBILE PHONES

The PlayStation version of Genshin Impact requires a minimum of 30GB free disk space. For mobile phones, especially android, please check our Mobile Performance page.
